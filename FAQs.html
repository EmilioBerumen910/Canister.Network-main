Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload my content?

Send a email to canisterwebpage@gmail.com, the Canister team will reach out to you with instructions on how to proceed with uploading your projects onto the platform. The Canister team will guide you through the necessary steps to ensure your films are successfully uploaded and shared on the platform.

As a content creator, do I receive revenue by uploading on the website?

No content creator receives any type of revenue by uploading content on a Canister.

Is Canister making any profit from my ShortFilms or Music?

Canister is a non-profit organization with the sole objective of sharing students' works and allowing them to reach new audiences. As a non-profit platform, Canister does not generate any profit from your short films or Music and operates solely to support and showcase the creative endeavors of Full Sail University students.

Is the platform limited to networking only for Full Sail University?

Yes, the platform is primarily focused on networking for Full Sail University students. However, it may also provide opportunities for showcasing and promoting their short films beyond the college community.
